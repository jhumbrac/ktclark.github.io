~ Travel Tip of the Day! ~

April 19, 2015:

When you're relying on public transport, always keep an eye on the clock.

There's absolutely no problem in walking a couple miles to where you're laying your head on any particular night. Especially in Europe, where people walk between destinations a lot more frequently than in the US. That being said, if you end up stranded 7 miles from your hostel at 2 in the morning in a thunderstorm because you weren't watching the clock to catch the last metro, you might regret a life choice or two. So, always be aware of when the last transport leaves, whether it's feasible to walk the distance instead, and what the time is if it's not feasible and there are no alternatives.
